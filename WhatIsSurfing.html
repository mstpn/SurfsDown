What is Surfing?

SurfsDown is about surfing in the video game Counter Strike: Global Offensive (CS:GO). Surfing refers to a set of custom maps that completely alter the game rules and take advantage of the unique physics in CS:GO’s Source engine to turn the first-person shooter into an extreme giant slalom. Instead of shooting your opponents, you slide down ramps at high velocities, navigating a course of jumps and obstacles to reach the finish line.
